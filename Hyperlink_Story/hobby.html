Hobby

3D Printing

3D printing is an exciting hobby that allows me to bring my digital designs to life. I enjoy exploring various design software and creating intricate models. It's fascinating to witness the transformation of a virtual concept into a physical object through the additive manufacturing process.

The picture show my current printing set up. To this day I still impressed on the fact that I manage to fit in 2 Aquila Voxelab C2 3d printer into the "enclosure" ie my closet. The 2 printer can run simultaneously without hindering each other performance using Octopi, this allow me to prototype for my project with double the speed.

Fighting Game

Fighting games have always been a source of enjoyment for me. Besides their entertainment value, they have also helped me in understanding cybersecurity tactics. These games require strategic thinking, anticipation, and adapting to opponents' moves, which parallels the mindset needed in cybersecurity to identify vulnerabilities, anticipate threats, and counteract them effectively.

Blazblue Central Fiction

Blazblue Central Fiction is a fast-paced 2D fighting game known for its complex mechanics and diverse roster of unique characters. It offers a rich storyline, intense battles, and a variety of gameplay modes that cater to both casual and competitive players.

Guilty Gear Xrd REV 2

Guilty Gear Xrd REV 2 is a visually stunning fighting game that combines stylish anime-inspired visuals with deep gameplay mechanics. It features a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique playstyle

Melty Blood: Code Lumina

Melty Blood: Code Lumina is a popular fighting game series known for its fast-paced and fluid combat system. It offers a unique blend of traditional 2D fighting mechanics with intricate character-specific mechanics and flashy special moves.

Plastic Modeling

Plastic modeling is a hobby that allows me to express my creativity and attention to detail. I enjoy assembling and painting plastic model kits, whether it's scale models of vehicles, aircraft, or fictional subjects. It's a relaxing and rewarding activity that combines craftsmanship and artistic expression.

The picture include most of my custom model kit from Bandai Spirits 30 minute mission model kit series(please note that the web link is in Japanese). This series allow modeller to custom their mecha model to their liking using modular and interchangeable part. I also hand paint most of the model myself using Decoart's acrylic water soluble paint series
