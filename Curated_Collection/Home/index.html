Research/Career Interests

My current interests revolve around three areas: Purple Teaming in cybersecurity, translating cybersecurity to Vietnamese, and gamifying cybersecurity. Purple Teaming involves combining offensive and defensive techniques for comprehensive security. Translating cybersecurity to Vietnamese aims to make it more accessible. Gamifying cybersecurity uses game elements to enhance training and awareness. These pursuits reflect my passion for continuous learning and practical application of cybersecurity principles.
