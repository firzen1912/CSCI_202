Epilog Fusion Pro Laser Cutter

The Epilog Fusion Pro Laser Cutter is an 80 watt, CO2 laser with a print bed size of 48" by 36". It can easily cut or engrave materials such as fabric, cardboard, wood, and acrylic up to 1/4" thick. The large work area allows for you to create large scale projects using a variety of different materials. In addition, we have a rotary attachment that allows for engraving on cylindrical objects such as water bottles.
